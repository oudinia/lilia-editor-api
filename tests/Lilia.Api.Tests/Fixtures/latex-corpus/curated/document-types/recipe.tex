\section*{Classic Tomato Pasta}

\textbf{Serves:} 4 \hfill \textbf{Prep:} 10 min \hfill \textbf{Cook:} 20 min

\subsection*{Ingredients}
\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
  \item 400g spaghetti
  \item 2 tbsp olive oil
  \item 3 cloves garlic, minced
  \item 1 can (400g) whole peeled tomatoes
  \item 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  \item Salt and black pepper to taste
  \item Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  \item Grated parmesan to serve
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Method}
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Bring a large pot of salted water to the boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package directions.
  \item Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add the garlic and pepper flakes; cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  \item Crush the tomatoes into the pan with your hands or a wooden spoon. Season with salt and pepper.
  \item Simmer the sauce for 10--15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  \item Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of pasta water.
  \item Toss the pasta with the sauce. Loosen with pasta water if needed.
  \item Serve topped with torn basil leaves and grated parmesan.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Tips}
\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
  \item For a smoother sauce, blend before tossing with pasta.
  \item Add a splash of cream for a richer variation.
\end{itemize}
