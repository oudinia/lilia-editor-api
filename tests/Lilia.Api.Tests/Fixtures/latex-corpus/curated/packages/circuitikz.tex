\section{Electrical circuits}

\begin{circuitikz}
  \draw (0,0) to[V, v=$U_s$] (0,3)
        to[R=$R_1$] (3,3)
        to[R=$R_2$] (3,0) -- (0,0);
\end{circuitikz}

A simple series circuit: voltage source $U_s$ in series with two resistors $R_1$ and $R_2$. Ohm's law gives $I = U_s / (R_1 + R_2)$.

\begin{circuitikz}
  \draw (0,0) to[C=$C_1$] (0,2) -- (3,2)
        to[L=$L_1$] (3,0) -- (0,0);
\end{circuitikz}

An LC oscillator with natural frequency $\omega_0 = 1/\sqrt{LC}$.
